# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Maximize your SaaS revenue with our intelligent revenue recovery platform that minimizes failed payments through smart retry timing, personalized dunning emails, and real-time error detection."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. "Transform your payment failures into revenue gains with our automated recovery solutions tailored for SaaS."
2. "Stop losing revenue to failed payments—our platform ensures timely retries and effective communication with your customers."
3. "Unlock hidden MRR by leveraging our intelligent revenue recovery tools designed specifically for SaaS companies."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Sarah the SaaS Founder**  
   - **Daily Job:** Overseeing product development and customer success, managing finances, and strategizing growth.  
   - **Budget:** $5,000/month for tools that enhance revenue management and customer retention.

2. **Persona 2: Mark the Finance Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Analyzing financial reports, managing cash flow, and optimizing revenue streams.  
   - **Budget:** $3,000/month for software that reduces payment failures and improves financial forecasting.

3. **Persona 3: Lisa the Customer Success Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Engaging with customers, resolving issues, and ensuring customer satisfaction and retention.  
   - **Budget:** $2,000/month for tools that enhance customer communication and reduce churn.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 per acquired customer through SEO-optimized blog posts, case studies, and whitepapers.

2. **Email Marketing**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 per acquired customer through targeted campaigns to existing SaaS companies, offering free trials and demos.

3. **Partnerships with SaaS Platforms**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per acquired customer through co-marketing initiatives and integrations with popular SaaS platforms.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing the revenue recovery platform; share on social media.
- **Week 2:** Release a case study on the impact of failed payments on SaaS revenue.
- **Week 3:** Host a webinar on best practices for managing payment failures and dunning strategies.
- **Week 4:** Publish an infographic illustrating the revenue recovery process and its benefits.
- **Week 5:** Share customer testimonials and success stories on social media.
- **Week 6:** Release an eBook on maximizing MRR through effective payment management.
- **Week 7:** Create a video tutorial on setting up the revenue recovery platform.
- **Week 8:** Conduct a Q&A session on social media to address common concerns about payment recovery.
- **Week 9:** Launch a blog post on the importance of personalized communication in dunning emails.
- **Week 10:** Share industry statistics on failed payments and their impact on SaaS companies.
- **Week 11:** Release a follow-up case study showcasing early adopters of the platform.
- **Week 12:** Host a roundtable discussion with industry experts on future trends in revenue recovery.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and begin beta testing with select customers.
- **D-0:** Official product launch; initiate marketing campaigns across selected channels.
- **D+30:** Gather user feedback, analyze initial performance metrics, and adjust marketing strategies as needed.
- **D+90:** Conduct a comprehensive review of product performance, customer acquisition, and revenue growth; prepare for scaling efforts.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target of 500 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Target:** Achieve $50,000 in MRR by D+90.
