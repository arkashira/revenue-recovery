# Customer Journey Map for Revenue Recovery Platform

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS company experiences a spike in failed payments and notices a drop in MRR.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about the impact of failed payments; difficulty in identifying the root cause of payment failures.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, concern about revenue loss.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content on the importance of payment recovery; offer a free assessment tool to analyze current payment failure rates.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits to educational resources.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS company researches solutions to reduce payment failures and improve revenue recovery.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of solutions available; unclear differentiation between products; skepticism about effectiveness.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious, feeling overwhelmed.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer clear comparisons with competitors; provide case studies showcasing successful implementations; free trials or demos.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests or trial sign-ups.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS company signs up for a trial of the revenue recovery platform.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; integration challenges with existing systems; learning curve associated with new features.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; concern about time investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide personalized onboarding support; offer easy-to-follow setup guides and video tutorials; proactive check-ins during the trial period.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate and time to first successful recovery.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS company successfully integrates the platform and begins to see improvements in payment recovery.
- **Friction Points**: Ongoing maintenance and updates; potential issues with customer support responsiveness.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief, and confidence in the solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular updates on performance metrics; personalized success stories; proactive customer support.
- **Metric**: Monthly recurring revenue growth attributed to the platform.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS company looks to scale and optimize further revenue recovery efforts.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about additional features or services; potential budget constraints for expansion.
- **User Emotions**: Ambition, eagerness, but also hesitation regarding investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer tailored expansion plans; provide insights on advanced features that can further enhance revenue recovery; loyalty discounts for long-term customers.
- **Metric**: Rate of upsell to premium features or services.
